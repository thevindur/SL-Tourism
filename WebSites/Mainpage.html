About Sri Lanka

Endless beaches, timeless ruins, welcoming people, oodles of elephants, rolling surf, cheap prices, fun trains, famous tea and flavourful food make Sri Lanka irresistible.

Sri Lanka is a mesmerizing island nation, South of India. Surrounded by the blue Indian Ocean from all sides, the island covers an area of 65,610 sq kms. Blessed with a diverse landscape ranging from lush green tropical forests to highlands, arid plains and pristine sandy beaches, this county has something for every one.

About Us

Our vision is to provide our tourists an idea about the country and if they decide to visit Sri Lanka we try to provide them all the information they require to successfully explore the country. We also offer a wide range of products to purchase which are unique to Sri Lanka.
